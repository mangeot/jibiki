Lookup

To find a word in the dictionary, use the central search form as follows:

  • Étape 1 : enter the word or prefix of the search word ;
  • Etape 2 : click the arrow button "→".

In the left bar appear the headwords of articles featured in previous and following in lexicographical order of the dictionary. You can also browse through the previous and following headwords that are not yet posted dragging the elevator top and bottom of the area.
